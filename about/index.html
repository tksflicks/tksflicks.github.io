TAEKYUNG KIM

I was born in Busan, Korea and raised in Colorado since the age of two. Living near the foothills of the Rockies, I lived in a city of mountains. It was a beautiful city made of not concrete but skycrapers of limestone and shale.

With more trees and rocky protrusions than people, my love for photography began with capturing the quiet landscape. But, through my years of taking photos I've found the joy in taking photos of the people around me!

Now, I primarily based in Atlanta, Georgia, I photograph to capture a new landscape of diverse culture, trees, and concrete skycrapers.
